ACCRA
ESPORTS FESTIVAL 2023

Our Elecronic Sports Festival brings together the broad community of Gamers who are willing to share knowledge, experience and skill. We hope that this Year extends interest in our industry beyond its members.

This festival also provides a platform for up-and-coming gamers to showcase their talents and potentially launch their careers. In addition to competition, we also offer workshops we call 'E-Talk' for attendees to learn from industry experts. We strive to promote inclusivity and diversity in the gaming community through our events and programming. We are excited to see the connections and friendships that will be formed at our festival, bringing the gaming community even closer together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Accra Esports Festival 2023 info@ghanaesportsfederation.org
