ACCRA
ESPORTS FESTIVAL
2023

An assembly of true gamers across all 16 regions of Ghana with a united passion of championing a the survival and growth of Electionic Sports.

2023.01.14 (Sat) ~ 15 (Sun)

@ Internation Conference Centre, Accra.

Main Program

E-Talk

An opportunity to discuss all components of Electronic Sports (Status, Challenges, Growth and Development) with Experts in the Ghanaian Space.

Master Class

Get invaluable Knowledge and Skill from the very best Esports Players, Teams and Coaches presesnt at the Festival in a One-on-One Class Session.

Exhibition

Various display of Games across generations and platforms for all gamers to try new games and revisit old games for free.

Tournament

A Giant pot-prized competition for interested E-sports Teams and Players.

E-Connect

A huge opportunity to Network with all people present including Gamers, Team Coaches, Team Scouts, Businessmen, National Esports Leaders etc.
